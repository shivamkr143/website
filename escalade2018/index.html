About Escalade

What is Escalade?

Escalade is the national Robotics competition conducted by the Robotics module of Techniche.

Where is it held?

This event is conducted in 12 cities across India.

What is in it for us?

Top 6 teams from each city are selected to participate in Escalade mains to be held in IIT Guwahati during Techniche 2018. Prizes worth Rs.80,000 are waiting just for you.

What should we do?

Metropolis has been wrecked in the recent battle between Doomsday and the Justice League. People are trapped inside buildings but can't get out. Until Batman can figure out a way to evacuate them, you have to construct a bot to deliver care packages to those people. Are you up for the task? Construct a bot to complete all the tasks given.
